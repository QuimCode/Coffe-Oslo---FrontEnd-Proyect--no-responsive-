    ORGANIC INGREDIENTS
    Our breads are made with love and dedication, but above all taking care of the health of our customers and the environment. They are based on renewable and wholemeal flour breads of the purest grains that can be found in the region, thanks to the purchase of grain seeds that go through a seasonal rotating crop every year, thus achieving that its purity is unmatched. and at the same time taking care of soil fertility.
